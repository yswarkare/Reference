Full stack FPRT question is attached below -
Duration - 24 hrs

Create a simple blog application where you can:  

1. SignUp, login and can manage your profile.
2. A dashboard where you have a listing of blogs and can see details of it.  
When not logged in you can perform below things:

1. Can only see the blogs written by other people.
2. Search for the blog.
When logged in you can perform below things:

1. Can create your own blog which others can see.
    (only text blogs is required, other kinds depends on you)
2. Follow other users(no request needed, can directly start following them)
3. The blogs from the people you are following will be listed first then of others.
4. Like, comment on blog, able to see other people's comments on the posts too.
